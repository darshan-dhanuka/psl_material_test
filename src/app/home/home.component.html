About Us

The idea behind Poker Sports League, is to sportify the game. To Introduce a league for team-based poker, the first and only of its kind. To provide an Arena which is fair and accessible to all. To provide a stage where the best, Nationally and Internationally can bring out the big guns and duke it out for the Glory. We have big prize pools, no entry fee and a hell of competition ahead. The league's only aim is to grow the game. To take it to heights previously unheard of. And you can rest assured we will leave no stone unturned while doing that.
