A pro player is a Marquee player who is supposed to give their team a tactical edge with high skill and finesse in execution. As such the following minimum requirements need to be met before applying.

1) Two years of experience as a professional poker player.

2) Playing actively for a year (800 tournaments online, 40 live, 500 tournaments a year if doing both)
