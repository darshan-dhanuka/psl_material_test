HOW TO PLAY

‘PSL is for all poker enthusiasts who dream of greatness. It’s a free entry league that aims to provide everyone who loves poker a shot of playing the biggest poker league in India. If you are someone who lives and breaths poker, here is your chance to prove your love for the game. ’

There are four roads that lead to the Poker sports league arena. need to be met before applying.

Online Qualifiers- Our online qualifiers are for poker enthusiasts who are unable to or can’t travel to our live qualifiers. There will be a total of 24 online qualifiers out of which a player is permitted to play a maximum of 6. X% of players will be awarded leaderboard points based on this formula *link*. Top players on the leaderboard will be awarded a spot in the Online qualifiers draft. This is the road for you if you are a poker amateur eager to prove your chops.

Live Qualifiers- Our Live qualifiers are for the newer players to land a spot in a team. They are held at premiere locations, providing a great battling experience where amateurs can duke it out for a spot in something bigger. Something in a “league” of it’s own. There will be a total of x live qualifiers out of which a player is permitted to play a maximum of 6. X% of players will be awarded leaderboard points based on this formula *link*. Top players on the leaderboard will be awarded a spot in the Online qualifiers draft. This is the road for you if you are a poker amateur eager to prove your chops while staring your opponent down!

Pro- A pro player is a Marquee player who is supposed to give their team a tactical edge with high skill and finesse in execution. As such the following minimum requirements need to be met before applying.

1) Two years of experience as a professional poker player.

2) Playing actively for a year (800 tournaments online, 40 live, 500 tournaments a year if doing both)

If, you meet the following criteria, please submit your application here

Once selected, you will be automatically drafted in the Pro Draft, where you will be picked by a team after deliberation between the Owner and the Mentor.

Mentors- Mentors are captains and in game leaders. They are there to give their team leadership and a strategic edge. They make the difficult calls, assign teammates roles and events and along with the owner take key decisions for their teams. It is a demanding role, therefore, you need to meet the following minimum requirements before applying.

1) Needs 5 years of experience as a professional poker player.

2) Needs to have previous leadership experience.

3) Needs to have played actively (500+ tournaments online) for a year.

4) Needs to have played at least 3000 tournaments in the last 5 years.

5) Needs to have at least 10 ITM’s in International fields.

If, you meet the following criteria, please submit your application here

You will be automatically drafted to the Mentor Draft,where the Team owners will pick their Mentors based on their personal preferences. If you are a veteran who wants to grow both the scene and the game, this is the road for you. Applications close on X, Selections to be made by Y.

Team Discretion-

Wildcard- A team may choose upto 2 wildcard players in their team, They are chosen at the discretion of the team and owner. They can range from an International pro to a local celebrity. You cannot apply for a wildcard entry. But if you think you can add value to a team by being one, you can approach the owner directly.
